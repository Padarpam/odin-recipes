Bolognese

Ingredients:

  • 2 Onions
  • 2 Celery stalks
  • 5 Cloves of garlic
  • 2 Red chilies de-seeded
  • 500-700 grams of mince
  • 800 grams of canned tomatoes
  • 2 teaspoons of tomato purée
  • Beef or Chicken stock
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oregano
  • Basil
  • Parmesan Cheese

Instructions:

  1. Chop onions, celery and garlic finely
  2. Add 2 tablespoons olive oil into the pan
  3. On medium high heat cook the onions, chilies and celery until translucent
  4. Add garlic and mince, stir with vigor
  5. Once the mince has browned add tomato purée, cook for 1 min
  6. Add canned tomatoes, stir with vigor
  7. Add a pinch of salt, pepper, oregano and some stock
  8. Leave on a low simmer with cracked lid for 40 minutes or more
  9. Boil pasta in salted water
  10. Add a scoop of pasta cooking water to the sauce
  11. Serve with grated parmesan and basil to taste
